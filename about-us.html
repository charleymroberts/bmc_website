About Us

The Peak Circuit consists of 12 chapels ranging from Woodlands in the north to Youlgrave in the south under the care of two ministers, Rev Adrian Perry at Bakewell and Rev Julie Letts at Hathersage. Bamford is part of the Hathersage section.

We celebrated our bicentenary in 2021. We seek to continue to serve our community, especially young people.
